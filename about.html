Our mission is to enable data-driven decisions in all organizations

DataMatrix NG is exclusively focused on providing solutions to unlock the value in your data and transform them into actionable insights. Our expert professionals have been providing data intelligence solutions to medium and large enterprises for over a decade across several industries.

We work with data of all sizes and connect to ANY enterprise data source residing inside the organization or in the cloud. Our Data Analytics platform uses technologies such as Hadoop, Hive, MapReduce and Storm as well as tools from select leading vendors to provide data visualizations-dashboards and reports, self-service data discovery and collaboration features enabling quick and seamless decision making.

At DataMatrix, we believe that everyone in all organizations should be able to get answers from their combined data sources provided in a easy, beautiful user experience.
