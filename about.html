Our mission is to enable data-driven decisions in all organizations

DataMatrix NG is exclusively focused on providing solutions to unlock the value in your data. Our expert professionals have been implementing business ntelligence solutions medium to large enterprises for over a decade.

We work with data of all sizes and integrate with any enterprise data source residing inside the organization or in the cloud. Our Data Analytics platform uses technologies such as Hadoop, Hive, MapReduce and Storm as well as tools with select leading vendors that provide data visualizations-dashboards and reports and collaboration features for quick and seamless decision making.

At DataMatrix, we believe that organizations should be able to get answers from their data.
